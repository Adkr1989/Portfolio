// The Uncommon Path

Most AI developers come from software. I came from crawl spaces, construction sites, and concert venues. I've operated heavy machinery at dawn and mixed live sound at midnight. I've walked through warehouses, production floors, and office buildings doing energy audits.

Every single place I've worked, I saw the same thing: inefficiency hiding in plain sight. Paper forms that could be automated. Tribal knowledge that walked out the door when someone quit. Compliance tracking done in spreadsheets that nobody trusted. I couldn't stop thinking about how technology could fix it.

Now I build those solutions. My current employer's grant tracking system? I built it. Their energy rebate calculator? Mine too. That's not a portfolio project - that's production code handling real money, solving problems I personally witnessed for years.
