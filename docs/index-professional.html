My Background

From Operational Inefficiency to AI Solutions

I've worked construction sites, energy audits, event production, and everything in between. At every job, I saw the same pattern: businesses drowning in manual processes, tribal knowledge, and outdated systems.

I couldn't stop thinking about it. The paper forms that should be digital. The compliance tracking done in spreadsheets nobody trusted. The expertise that disappeared when employees left. I saw these problems everywhere - and I knew technology could solve them.

That frustration became my mission. Now I build AI systems that solve the exact problems I witnessed firsthand. My current employer's grant tracking? I built it. Their rebate calculator? Mine too. Real solutions for real problems I spent years watching go unsolved.

My diverse background isn't a detour - it's my competitive advantage. I understand how businesses actually operate, not just how they should work in theory.
